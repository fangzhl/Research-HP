Background:Metallic glass gear and magnetic core

Applications of metallic glass gears and magnetic core in aerospace & EV industries are the essential motivation

・Why Metallic glass?

・Advantages: High strength, hardness, elastic, corrosion resistance and magnetic property
・Downsides for manufacturing: Difficult-to-cut material, severe tool wear, embrittlement and magnetic deterioration with crystalization, micro-level thickness (metallic glass foil).

Metallic glass: laminate structure

Superior material for next-generation magnetic core: Iron-based metallic glass foil.
